Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer. He was born on 24 April 1973 in Bombay (now Mumbai), Maharashtra. He is also know as Tendlya, Little Master and Master Blaster. He is a right-handed batsman and right-arm medium, leg break and off break bowler. Ramesh Tendulkar is the father, Rajni Tendulkar is the mother, Anjali Tendulkar is the wife, Sara Tendulkar is the daughter and Arjun Tendulkar is the son of Sachin Tendulkar. He made his International debut on 15 November 1989 against Pakistan at the age of sixteen in Karachi and announced his retirement from all forms of cricket on 16 November 2013.
